The recipe for butter cake:
210g Caster sugar
250g Butter unsalted
200g Self-raising flour
3 Large free range eggs
185g Milk full cream
1 Tbsp Honey + extra to brush on later
2 Tbsp Extra virgin olive oil
2 pinches of Sea salt
180C Oven
15-20 minutes
Citrus or other fruits of your choice to top.
